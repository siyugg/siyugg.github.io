Javascript React Native (iOS) Solidity Truffle Etherum (ERC 721)
A decentralized application (dApp) designed to revolutionize the way we verify the authenticity of physical products. By integrating blockchain technology, Authentic8 enables each product to be tagged with a unique QR code, serving as a digital passport throughout its lifecycle.
